### Root Cause: Post-Plan Deviation in Commit 855676e

The initial implementation (commit 24187bf) was correct and exactly matched the plan:
- `transition-transform duration-300 ease-in-out`
- `open ? 'translate-y-0' : 'translate-y-full'`

Commit `855676e` ("fix(02.1): adjust classnames on PrivacyNotice and TrademarkNotice panels") introduced two regressions:

1. Changed `transition-transform` to `transition-all` — this transitions ALL animatable properties, not just transform, causing performance overhead and animating any unintended property changes.
2. Added `inset-100` to the open state. In Tailwind v4 with default `--spacing: 0.25rem`, `inset-100` computes to `inset: calc(0.25rem * 100) = inset: 25rem`. This applies a 25rem inset on all four sides when the panel is open. With `transition-all`, this inset also animates on open/close, producing an incorrect animation on the panel position rather than a clean translate-y slide.

### Gaps Summary

The phase was initially implemented correctly (commit 24187bf), then a follow-up commit (855676e) introduced two regressions into both PrivacyNotice.tsx and TrademarkNotice.tsx: replacing `transition-transform` with `transition-all`, and adding a stray `inset-100` class to the open state. In Tailwind v4, `inset-100` resolves to `inset: 25rem` (all four sides), which incorrectly repositions the panel when open and animates via `transition-all`. Both files need `transition-all` reverted to `transition-transform` and `inset-100` removed from the open branch.

The fix is mechanical: in each component, on the panel `div`:
- Change `transition-all` to `transition-transform`
- Change `open ? 'translate-y-0 inset-100' : 'translate-y-full'` to `open ? 'translate-y-0' : 'translate-y-full'`
